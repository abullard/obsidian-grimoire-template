### [[MoC - Grimoire|← Parent]]

> [!Tip] Capture TODOs Anywhere
> Use Tasks to keep track of outstanding items anywhere in your Grimoire.
> 
> Surface those TODOs at whatever scope you'd like.
> 
> 📖 For more information, read the "Tasks" plugin's [Documentation](https://publish.obsidian.md/tasks/Introduction)
## Capture a Task
The syntax is easy:
```Markdown
- [ ] #todo <task-goes-here>
```

In practice–with various metadata:
- [ ] #todo Bake Grandma some cookies 🔺
- [ ] #todo Buy Grandpa a Shake Weight 📅 03-21-2067 🔼  
- [ ] #todo Hand-to-hand combat with a real life snake 🔁 every week on Sunday ⏫ 
- [ ] #todo Manipulate Keanu Reeves into being mean just once 🆔 oc4lyw 
- And more!
## Surface Tasks

> [!example] 
> Reference [[MoC - Tasks]] to see a live example.

You can scope these to whatever file, folder, heading, etc. you want:
```tasks
not done
path includes {{query.file.path}}
group by backlink
hide backlink
```
